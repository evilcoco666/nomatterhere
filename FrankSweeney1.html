People Enjoy my Company by Frank Sweeney


'People enjoy my company' explores the 1999 privatisation of Telecom Éireann from the viewpoint of shareholders communicating on early online forums. The film contextualises this event within emerging ideologies of technological emancipation in the pre millennium period.


Film by Frank Sweeney
Animation by Andrew Loughnane
